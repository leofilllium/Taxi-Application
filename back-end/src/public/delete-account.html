Safar One Account Deletion Request

This page is managed by Safar One Team (Developer: Rafis Kabirov).

How to request account deletion

  1. Enter your User ID in the form below.
  2. Optionally provide a reason for deletion.
  3. Click Submit Request.
  4. Our team will verify your identity and delete your account within 7 business days.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data Deletion Policy

Data that will be deleted:

  • Profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Booking and payment history
  • Saved preferences and travel data

Data that may be retained temporarily:

  • Transaction data required by law (for tax and billing purposes) may be kept for up to 90 days.

After this period, all data will be permanently removed from our servers.
